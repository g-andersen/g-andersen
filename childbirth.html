Congrats! You're almost there!

As a first time parent, childbirth can sound pretty scary and overwhelming. Be sure to read up or take classes about the experience to help ease the uncertainty.

It is time to pack your hospital bag. Here are some items that you may find useful:

Please note that not all of these items are necessary and are for comfort and convenience. In fact, the hospital provides some of these items. Do what makes the most sense for you!

For Mom:

  • Photo ID and insurance info
  • Birth plan
  • Cell phone + charger (an extra long cord)
  • Non-skid socks
  • Warm robe or sweater
  • Toiletries (showering, comfort, etc)
  • Ponytails
  • Snacks
  • Nursing bras
  • Loose clothing
  • Going home outfit (think comfortable)
  • Bath towel
  • Angled peri bottle
  • Your own pillow
  • Postpartum recovery kit
  • Stool softener
  • Breast pump
  • Pajamas
  • Underwear
  • Extra bag to bring hospital extras home

For Baby:

  • Sleepers and onesies
  • Wipes and diapers (if you prefer ones other than what the hospital provides)
  • Weather appropriate going home outfit
  • Announcement swaddle
  • Socks and booties
  • Carseat
  • Receiving blanket

For Partner:

  • Toiletries
  • Keep busy activities
  • Snacks
  • Camera
  • Extra clothing
  • Cell phone and charger
  • Medications
  • Pillow and blanket
