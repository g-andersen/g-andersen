Things that you may want to consider having for your nursery:

Please note that not all of these items are necessary. Do what makes the most sense for you, your finances, and your baby.

  • Crib and/or bassinet
  • Crib mattress
  • Crib sheets
  • Baby monitor
  • Rocking chair
  • Nightlight or dimmable lamp
  • Dresser
  • Changing table (changing station can just go on dresser)
  • Bookshelf
  • Toy bins
  • Rug
