Where should I register?

There are so many options for where you can register. It really is personal preference. Consider accessibility, selection, and completion discount rate.

  • Amazon
  • Target
  • Crate & Kids
  • Walmart
  • Babylist
  • The Bump

Things that you may want to consider having on your registry:

This can get very overwhelming. Please note that not all of these items are necessary. Do what makes the most sense for you, your finances, and your baby.

  • Crib sheets

  • Travel crib

  • Sweaters and jackets

  • Receiving/stroller blankets
