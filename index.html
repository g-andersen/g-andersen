Welcome to My Website!

Hi! So you're pregnant... now what? You're likely feeling a lot of overwhelming emotions. This website is to help you have a starting place to organize your thoughts.

Navigating This Website

Trimesters
The "Trimesters" tab will give you the short and sweet on each trimester in your pregnancy.
Nursery
The "Nursery" tab will give you some pointers on things you may want to consider putting in your nursery.
Registry
The "Registry" tab is similar to the "Nursery" tab, and will provide you with a list of things that may be useful on your registry.
Childbirth
Lastly, the "Childbirth" tab will provide information about a hospital birth experience and what to pack in your hospital bag. Enjoy!
